1. Location Information

We may collect precise location information When the BioMediss app is running in the foreground (app open and on-screen) or background (app open but not on-screen) to facilitate attendance and visit entries during the user's office or work time only. This information is solely used for the intended purpose and is not shared with any third party. We request notification permissions to inform you about the collection of background location data.

We may request access to your device's camera and photos to allow you to upload your profile photo. This permission is entirely optional and is not required for the core functionality of the Application. 3. User Profile Data:

The Application allows users to update their profile data, including but not limited to their Name, Phone number, and alternative number. It is important to note that providing this information is entirely optional. Users may choose to update their profile with any combination of these details, and they can opt not to provide any information if they prefer.

Users also have the option to upload a profile photo. This feature is entirely optional, and users are not required to provide a profile photo to use the Application. If a user chooses to upload a profile photo, it will be displayed within the Application's interface for personalization purposes.

All profile data provided by users, including name, phone number, alternative number, and profile photo (if uploaded), will be stored securely within the Application.
